Restaurant Description

Tom's Bistro is a classy, authentic, new-world Italian restaurant in the heart of Pawnee's lowertown restaurant district. With a menu of tender pastas, fresh sauces, and a full wine list, Tom's Bistro makes a great date night, pre-party spot, or wine bar to meet and chat.
